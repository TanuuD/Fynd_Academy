FAQ

How does this benefit my business?

The app lets you reduce your food waste and earn back money on stock that would have been thrown away - a win-win. Further still, 76% of customers who discover a store through the app will return as full-paying customers.

How much extra work does this create for my business?

Almost no more than it takes to throw food away! All that is required of you is to set aside your surplus to be picked up by a customer. Then, at the pickup time, the customer shows their digital receipt and you hand over the food.

How much does it cost?

Signing up to the app is free, and you won't pay anything unless you sell food through the app. Once you start selling food, we'll deduct an annual fee and a small commission on each dish and Surprise Bag sold. This commission fee varies depending on what you charge for your Surprise Bags. Reach out to our team, and we'll walk you through it.

How do I get paid?

We'll send you the money you've earned from the app on a quarterly basis. All you have to do is give us your bank account details - you can do this using MyStore on the app or on desktop.
